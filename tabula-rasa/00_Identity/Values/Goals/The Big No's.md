# What Should not Be Done:

I mean to describe the following activities which are poison for a person, most critically of all they do not even feel like a poison; you feel relief, pleasure, enjoyment, peace. But it isn't real, it's artificial and inflated.


| Activity                                              | Reasoning                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 | Exceptions                                                                                                                             |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Alcohol                                               | Damages the liver. Clouds the mind and judgement, causes poor decisions to be made. Sows the seeds of addiction                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           | Key events - Child births, death of a family member. When the drink serves a real purpose.                                             |
| Gaming                                                | You develop capacities for an activities that are endless and everchanging, until they cease to become enjoyable anymore, then you move on to the next game or become bitter and addicted to one. The danger is in building skills that are pointless and wasting youthfulness.                                                                                                                           | If in person/ couch coop/arcade. When the gaming serves a valuable **in-person** social purpose.                                       |
| Social Media                                          | You lose touch with what is real and what isn't. You start to think and consider and talk about things which are misinformed or untrue, since that is what it popular. You build up a cynical narrative and become a consumer rather than a creator. The comparison culture is what crushes a person's sense of worth.                                                                                    | If used for professional purposes. Think career, business, etc.                                                                        |
| YouTube/Short-form Content/Reels/Tiktoks/etc          | Destroys the ability to focus. Attention is spent on meaningless activities, you misinform yourself and those around you. You become a watcher rather than a doer.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | If you are the one building or making the content, then it is acceptable.                                                              |
| Smoking/Vaping                                        | Damages the lungs, creates desire and need for a substance that just takes away time, money, and energy.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | No.                                                                                                                                    |
| Street Drugs                                          | Similarly to smoking/vaping.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              | No.                                                                                                                                    |
| Gambling                                              | You are taught and pushed towards the unsustainable path of quick returns or growth. Which is nothing more than a fabrication, you will have hot streaks and cold streaks. But the house will always win, and will help you crush yourself for their profit - with a smile and helping hand.                                                                                                              | No.                                                                                                                                    |
| Food Addiction / UPFs / Obesity                       | You become weak and feeble. Human's are not suppose to be fat balls of lard, it may happen to a person and it is disgusting. There is nothing wrong with being overweight, but choosing to remain like that is a horrendous person to be around. It shows that a person has given up. What hurts more is that the systems in place build this, people are unwillingly and unknowing brought to slaughter. | If someone else has cooked or made something for you, or the purchasing of said food is outside of your control.                       |
| Pride / Arrogance                                     | Just because you know or see things that other people don't see. Doesn't mean you are any better, nor does it give you the right to walk over them or disregard them. If you can see clearly but walk the path of the blind what does it matter, if you walk the better path - then just walk the better path - the words and energy are cheap and are better spent raising up rather than tearing down.  | Unless something is truly worth protecting or fighting for. But don't let the ego tell you that something is important when it is not. |
| Anger / Rage                                          | Bad things, unfair things are going to happen. When you feel that frustration building up, that means it's time to go exercise to vent that stress. You must address it without cutting down your relationships and life. Not letting it consume or control you, but you harness it.                                                                                                                      | It is best to channel frustration, vitriol, and other negativity in a way which is positive, or to move on. It cannot be avoided.      |
| Overanalyses / Overthinking                           | Once you have a solution, or know what it right do it. Don't invent more and more jargon ontop.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           | When a decision requires deep analysis because it is very important.                                                                   |
| Procrastination / Novelty Seeking / Boredom avoidance | It is far too easy to research about topics or people or events that matter little. Ask yourself before you search or look up anything. What is the point of this? Does it make my life better? Does it matter in the long-run. 90% of things don't, yet they scream at you saying that the are. The other 10% sit quietly and wait, which are revealed to you if you listen.                             | Focus on your 10%. But use other people as novelty and seek new perspectives and experiences from them.                                |
| External Validation / Virtue Signalling               | Nobody cares about you. What someone says about you matters for appearances, but internally very rarely matters. Yourself should immerge from you. Don't seek praise, recognition, admiration, etc. Accept it politely if it comes.                                                                                                                                                                       | No. Be aware that most do require this and deal it out accordingly. But not sycophantically so.                                        |
| Avoiding discomfort                                   | Life has never been about being comfortable or easy. Stop treating it as such, your ancestors struggled everyday to survive and found happiness in that. Suffering will happen, embrace it, walk through it in a controlled manner, like a plunge into an icy lake, don't rush but slowly and carefully swim through.                                                                                     | Don't make yourself uncomfortable without purpose. But don't shy away from it. I.e. don't make yourself miserable.                     |
| Perfectionism                                         | Nothing will ever be perfect. No system. No invention. No man. Give yourself the benefit of the doubt and the slack, but return to the path or your purpose right away. Be compassionate, but don't dillydally. The world will beat you up, no need your beat yourself up more.                                                                                                                           | No. It never helps - good enough is 99% of the time good enough. And if it isn't, so be it.                                            |
| Fantasy / Future thinking / lack of present           | Enjoy said moment at hand. Staying locked up in your mind or a future will inflate your sense of what you should or shouldn't have. Nothing of the sort is guaranteed. Eat some bread, drink some water - ground yourself.                                                                                                                                                                                | When your path has not been defined, think about it - once you've written it down, execute it.                                         |
