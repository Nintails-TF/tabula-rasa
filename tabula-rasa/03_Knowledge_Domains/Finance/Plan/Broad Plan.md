# Consulting & Professional Services:

1. **Policy Translation** - New regulations every 2-4 years = companies need help adapting
2. **Political Cover** - Executives hire consultants to justify unpopular decisions
3. **Complexity Navigation** - More rules, more stakeholders, more confusion = more fees

If policies and laws change or if companies need to change, then consulting firms will be brought in to make hard decisions for a these companies if the leaders can't do them or can't get permissions from a board to do them.
