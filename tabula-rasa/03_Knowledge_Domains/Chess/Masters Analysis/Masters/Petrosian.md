# Tigran Petrosian

## Quick Reference
**Era:** Classical (1950s-1970s) | **World Champion:** 1963-1969 | **Style:** Defensive/Prophylactic

## Style & Principles
Defensive genius with unmatched danger-sensing ability. Master of prophylaxis—preventing opponent's plans before starting own. Created "deserts" where nothing could happen, then exploited microscopic advantages. Misunderstood as boring but incredibly sophisticated. Exceptional in blocked positions.

**Core Strengths:** Prophylaxis • Defense • Sensing danger • Positional restrictions
**Study Focus:** Preventing opponent ideas • Defensive resources • Patient waiting
