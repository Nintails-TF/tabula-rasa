# Practice Routine:

Pick 2-3 items from the [[Practice Menu]], ration out around 45-60 mins as a practice block, and expand or contract when necessary:

## Structure

- **Warmup:** 5 minutes
	- LG Duels or Aim Training
- **Drill:** 25 minutes
	- Work on improving the current weakness.
- **Apply / Flex:** 20 minutes
	- Apply what was practiced in the drill under more pressure. 
	- If not possible or applicable, move to a secondary drill/mechanics test/VOD study, etc.
- **Log:** 5-10 minutes
	- Log session results
	- Give reflective judgment on how session went
	- Set intention for next session.
