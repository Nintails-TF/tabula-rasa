# Plan

**Training** Strength x4 per week — compound lifts, shoulders prioritized, core built in. 60–75 minutes. Easy cardio x3 per week on off days — Zone 2 pace, 45–60 minutes. Walk instead if fatigued. Yoga daily — 20–30 minutes, adjusted to how tight or loose you feel that day.

**Diet** 0.8–1g protein per pound of bodyweight daily. Whole foods majority of the time — lean proteins, vegetables, whole grains, healthy fats. Stay hydrated. Moderate alcohol and processed food. No need to obsess — eat mindfully and consistently.

**Sleep** 8–9 hours. Non-negotiable. This is when the body actually changes.

**Weekly Structure** Mon — Lift Tue — Easy cardio Wed — Lift Thu — Easy cardio or walk Fri — Lift Sat — Easy cardio Sun — Lift or rest Yoga every day woven around how you feel.

**Timeline** 8–12 weeks — noticeable change. 6 months — genuinely impressive physique and fitness. 12 months — complete transformation physically, mentally, and physiologically. Habits become identity.

**Duration:** 14 hours per week or so.
