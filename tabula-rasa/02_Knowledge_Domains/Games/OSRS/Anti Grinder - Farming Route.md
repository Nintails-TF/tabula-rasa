# The Anti-Grinder OSRS Plan

The goal is a self-funding OSRS account built around discipline and consistency rather than long grinding sessions. The target is 99 Farming untrimmed, achieved purely through daily passive runs while staying bond-funded throughout.

## Herb Farm Runs:

Goal is to start farming with 7 patches, After My Arm's Big Adventure. Then 8 after 65 Farming.

- **Ardougne:** Skills Necklace (Fishing Guild)
- **Falador:** Ring of Wealth (Draynor Manor) or Chronicle
- **Catherby:** Camelot Teleport Tabs
- **Morytania:** Ectophial
- **Hosidius:** Xeric's Talisman (Glade)
- **Troll Stronghold:** Stony Basalt (or standard Troll Stronghold tele)
- **Farming Guild:** Skills Necklace (once 65)

Check with the farming calculator on what seeds to farm:

1. Ranarr - Classic but expensive seeds best used in disease-free plots.
2. Toadflax - Cheap and effective.
3. Kwuarm - Like Toadflax sometimes edges it out.
