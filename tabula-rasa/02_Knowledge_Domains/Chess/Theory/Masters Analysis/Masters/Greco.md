# Gioachino Greco

## Quick Reference
**Era:** Italian School (1600s) | **World Champion:** Never (pre-championship era) | **Style:** Tactical/Attacking

## Style & Principles
Early master who compiled one of the first chess books showing tactical patterns and combinations. Emphasized rapid development, centre control, and direct attacking play. His games demonstrate fundamental tactical motifs that remain relevant today. Known for "Greco's Sacrifice" patterns. Games are short, forcing, and instructive for learning basic attacking principles.

**Core Strengths:** Centre domination • Tactical patterns • Direct attacks • Piece activity
**Study Focus:** Centre control importance • Basic tactical motifs • Converting space advantage
