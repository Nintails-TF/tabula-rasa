# Hummus

Smooth, creamy hummus that comes together in minutes with a blender or food processor.

INGREDIENTS
• 400 grams canned chickpeas, drained (reserve the liquid)
• 3 tablespoons tahini
• 1 Lemon squeezed
• 1 garlic clove Or more if you like garlic.
• 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more to serve
• 0.5 teaspoons salt
• 3 tablespoons reserved chickpea liquid (aquafaba) or water
• 0.5 teaspoons ground cumin

STEPS
1. Blend base: Add 1 garlic clove, 3 tablespoons tahini, and 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice to a food processor or blender. Blend for about 1 minute until smooth and creamy.
2. Add chickpeas: Add the 425 grams canned chickpeas, drained (reserve the liquid), 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more to serve, 0.5 teaspoons salt, and 0.5 teaspoons ground cumin. Blend for another minute.
3. Adjust consistency: With the blender running, drizzle in the 3 tablespoons reserved chickpea liquid (aquafaba) one tablespoon at a time until you reach your desired creamy consistency. Blend for 2–3 more minutes for the smoothest result.
4. Taste and serve: Taste and adjust salt or lemon juice as needed. Spread into a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and optionally top with a pinch of paprika or fresh parsley.

NOTES

**Storage:** Keeps in the fridge in an airtight container for up to 5 days.
