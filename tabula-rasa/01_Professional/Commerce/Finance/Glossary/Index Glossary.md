## A:

### Investment Vehicles & Products:
- [[Terms Reference/A/Accumulating ETF]] - ETFs that automatically reinvest dividends for compound growth
- [[Terms Reference/A/American Depositary Receipt]] (ADR) - Certificates representing foreign company shares on US exchanges

### Performance & Metrics:
- [[Terms Reference/A/Alpha]] - Measure of investment performance vs. benchmark (excess return)
- [[Terms Reference/A/Alpha Decay]] - Gradual erosion of a strategy's excess returns over time
- [[Terms Reference/A/Annualized Return]] - Average annual rate of return accounting for compounding
- [[Terms Reference/A/Appreciation]] - Increase in asset value over time

### Corporate Actions & Governance
- [[Terms Reference/A/Acquisition]] - Purchasing another company for strategic expansion
- [[Terms Reference/A/Annual General Meeting]] (AGM) - Yearly shareholder meeting for voting and approval
- [[Terms Reference/A/Annual Report]] - Comprehensive yearly publication of company financials and strategy

### Trading & Market Mechanics
- [[Terms Reference/A/Active Management]] - Portfolio management attempting to beat benchmark indices
- [[Terms Reference/A/Aggregating Mode]] - Single position management with averaged entry prices
- [[Terms Reference/A/Arbitrage]] - Exploiting price discrepancies across markets for profit
- [[Terms Reference/A/Ask Price]] - Minimum price sellers will accept for a security
- [[Terms Reference/A/Ask Size]] - Quantity of shares available at the ask price

### Pricing Models & Theory
- [[Terms Reference/A/Arbitrage Pricing Theory]] (APT) - Multi-factor asset pricing model

### Portfolio Management
- [[Terms Reference/A/Asset Allocation]] - Strategic distribution across multiple asset classes
- [[Terms Reference/A/Asset Classes]] - Categories of investments with similar characteristics
- [[Terms Reference/A/Assets]] - Resources with economic value owned by individuals or businesses
- [[Terms Reference/A/Average Price Adjustments]] - Modifying cost basis by buying at different prices

### Tax & Retirement
- [[Terms Reference/A/After-tax Contribution]] - Money contributed post-tax (e.g., Roth IRA)
- [[Terms Reference/A/Alternative Minimum Tax]] (AMT) - Parallel tax ensuring minimum tax payment
- [[Terms Reference/A/Annual Contribution Limits]] - Maximum allowed contributions to tax-advantaged accounts
- [[Terms Reference/A/Annual Tax Statement]] - Summary of income and taxes for filing purposes

### Accounting & Finance
- [[Terms Reference/A/Amortisation]] - Gradual debt repayment or spreading intangible asset costs

### Employee Compensation
- [[Terms Reference/A/Award Agreement]] - Legal document detailing equity compensation terms
- [[Terms Reference/A/Award Price]] - Exercise price for stock options or equity awards
