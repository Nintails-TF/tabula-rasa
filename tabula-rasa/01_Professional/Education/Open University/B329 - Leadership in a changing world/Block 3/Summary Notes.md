**Leadership Style**
- Emergent authority through competence: organisation, communication, domain knowledge
	- Keeping notes, suggesting deadlines/meetings, being warm and professional, knowledge of the subject and cybersecurity.
- Facilitative rather than directive: creating structure, then stepping back
	- Set the conditions for people to perform, make it easier for them, remove the overhead - they can focus on turning up, thinking about the problem, and performing.
- Deliberate restraint: listening, asking questions, speaking when substantive
	- Not perfect, still talking a bit too much in depth about certain aspects of cybersecurity. It builds others confidence in me, but it also builds the assertion that they'll want me do the heavy lifting for the group work wise.
- Philosophy of bounded responsibility: build conditions for success, remain available, but accept others' contributions are their own choice
