# **No House, No Hope, No Problem?**

![[../../../../03_Reference/Pictures/perseipolis.png]]
*Source: Vieira (2004)*

Zero-hour contracts. Algorithmic bosses. Unaffordable housing. Welcome to the expendable generation - where stability is a luxury and young people work harder for less, with no voice in the systems that exploit them.

Young people in the UK face unprecedented *precarity* - the condition of living with uncertainty, instability, and vulnerability outside of their control. Destiny is slipping from their grasp; as businesses, governments, and economic forces hold the reigns of youth future and wellbeing.
