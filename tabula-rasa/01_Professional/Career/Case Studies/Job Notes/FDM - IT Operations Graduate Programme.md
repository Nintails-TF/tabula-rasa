## Company Status Report:

FDM is a "Recruit, Train, Deploy" business model. Meaning they source talent and sell them to companies. It's the typical body shop model, juniors are leased out to various companies to do work for them. Sometimes companies recognise the value of the junior and want to hire them after their contract ends.

Contracts are typically 2 years. Contracts can include exit fees, so be very careful what you sign.

Not the perfect place to work, but it would be something. If I could leverage the training and gaining experience, it could work out well.
