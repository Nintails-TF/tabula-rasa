# Questions:

- Can you tell us about why you were particularly drawn to this opportunity, and what made you apply?
- Can you tell us a bit more about what you like about Dumfries and Galloway? And what you think could be improved?
- In what ways do you think the young people of the Youth Advisory Forum could have a positive impact on the South of Scotland?
- Do you have any specific skills you’d like to develop or gain through being part of the Youth Advisory Forum and SOSE more generally?
- It was interesting to read about your passion for Chess and your involvement in Dumfries Chess Club. How did you find out about the club and what more do you think can be done to better promote and publicise such groups?
- What in particular do you enjoy most about running around Eskrigg nature reserve and do you think we make enough of our green spaces and nature to promote the region?
- Are there any other areas in your application that you’d like to take this opportunity to expand on?
- Availability – can you tell us more about your current commitments, and when you would prefer to take part in the Forum? Day/evening/weekdays/weekends?
