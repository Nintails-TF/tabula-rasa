## Connecting to Scenarios of Academic Integrity:

What is our ground rules? 

1. Partnership is relational, not procedural
2. Mutual respect and recognition of different forms of expertise
3. Shared responsibility and co-ownership
4. Explicit attention to power
5. Openness, uncertainty, and willingness to learn
6. Dialogue and active listening
7. Reflection as a continuous practice
8. Recognition of emotional and affective dimensions
9. Willingness to engage with difficulty, not just success
10. Contextual and ethical sensitivity

Key themes:

1. Listening and paying attention to others.
2. Curiosity and asking questions
3. Being supportive and helping each other.

4. Punctuality and showing up on time.
